**Handy tools I use every day to be a faster, sharper QA**

---

## 📸 Shottr

[shottr.cc](https://shottr.cc)  
A blazing-fast, lightweight screenshot tool for macOS.

> ✔️ Capture screenshots with pixel precision  
> ✔️ Annotate instantly  
> ✔️ Measure elements on screen  
> ✔️ Capture scrolling pages


---

## 🎥 LICEcap

[licecap](https://www.cockos.com/licecap/)  
A super-simple screen recorder that exports directly to GIF.

> 📌 Ideal for quick bug reproduction GIFs  
> 📌 Adjustable recording area  
> 📌 Lightweight and free


---

## 📱 Responsively App

[responsively.app](https://responsively.app)  
A free, open-source desktop app for responsive web testing.

> 🖥️ See your website on multiple device screens side-by-side  
> 🖱️ Simultaneous mirroring — interact with all devices at once  
> ⚡ Live reload, device presets, and custom viewport sizes  
> 🎯 Super handy for cross-device layout checks and mobile testing

---

## 📝 Obsidian

[obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md)  
A Markdown-based knowledge base with graph view.

> 📓 Use it for:
- Test notes  
- Bug reporting templates  
- QA checklists  

---
## 🌐 Must-Have Chrome Extensions

- 🕵️ **Inspecta** — Inspect,compare and update CSS visually
- 🎨 **ColorZilla** — Pick colors directly from any web page  
- 🔍 **WhatFont** — Identify fonts used on websites quickly  
- 📱 **Responsive Viewer** — Test your site on multiple device screen sizes in one view
