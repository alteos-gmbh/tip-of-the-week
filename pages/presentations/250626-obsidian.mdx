A Powerful Offline Knowledge Base
**A Daily Tool in My Workflow**

Obsidian is a free, offline-first note-taking app that stores your notes as local Markdown files. It lets you link notes, use tags, and extend functionality with plugins like Kanban boards, Canvas, and Excalidraw. Obsidian is a powerful, privacy-focused alternative to cloud tools like Notion, ideal for building a personal knowledge base.

---

## What is Obsidian?

* Obsidian is a free, powerful note-taking and personal knowledge management app.
* It stores your notes as local **Markdown files** on disk.
* Designed for flexibility, privacy, and extensibility.
* A great **free alternative** to Notion and similar systems.

---

## Why I Chose Obsidian

* I use Obsidian **daily** as my main knowledge base.
* It fits both structured and unstructured information.
* Enables deep customization and ownership of my data.
* I love that it's **completely free** and **works offline**.
